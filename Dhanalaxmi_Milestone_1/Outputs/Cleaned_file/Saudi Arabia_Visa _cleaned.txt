accessibility, making the country a much more attractive travel destination. types of saudi arabia visas for indians saudi arabia issues several visa types based on the purpose of travel, and each type has its own cost, validity, and document requirements.  tourist visa: this visa allows indian citizens to explore saudi arabia for leisure, cultural, or religious tourism (excluding hajj). typically valid for 1 year with multiple entries, each stay can last up to 90 days. documents required include a valid passport, visa application, travel online insurance, and proof of financial resources.  business visa: issued for official work, meetings, trade shows, or business conferences. business visas can be single or multiple-entry, with validity ranging from 3 months to 1 year. supporting documents usually include a letter of invitation from a saudi company, passport copies, and visa application forms.  visit visa: intended for indians visiting family or friends residing in saudi arabia. these can be single or multiple-entry visas, usually valid for 3 months, with an option to extend under certain conditions. proof of relationship, such as family certificates or sponsorship. the validity matches the employment contract, generally 12 years, and can be extended. additional requirements include medical clearance and attested educational or professional documents.  hajj and umrah visa: specifically for religious purposes, these visas are only valid during the pilgrimage period and cannot be used for other travel. applicants need a confirmed hajj/umrah package, passport, and vaccination certificates.  student visa: issued to indians enrolling in saudi educational institutions. validity depends on the duration of the course and enrollment status. students must provide an admission letter, visa application, and passport.  residence visa (iqama): this visa is for indians intending to stay long-term in saudi arabia, usually for work or
