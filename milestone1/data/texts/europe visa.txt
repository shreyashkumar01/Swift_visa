Checklist for Schengen Visa: Tourist 
Application should be submitted at the local Visa Facilitation Centre VFS. Switzerland Visa Application Centre Website: 
https://www.vfsglobal.ch/switzerland/india/index.html 
The visa application form is free of charge and can be downloaded from the website: 
https://www.sem.admin.ch/sem/en/home/themen/einreise/visumantragsformular.html 
Please submit the documents in the exact order given below, please do not staple any document. 
Valid national passport : 
o Issued within the last 10 years and with at least 3 months validity after the scheduled return, with at least two blank 
pages. Handwritten passports and passports with observations regarding the front page (bio data) cannot be accepted. 
1 passport size picture: 
o 
o 
White background, not older than 6 months (size 35 – 40 mm in width, not copied or scanned picture). 
Picture to be pasted on the application form, stapled or pinned pictures cannot be accepted. For more details, please 
refer to: http://www.icao.int/Security/mrtd/Downloads/Technical%20Reports/Annex_A-Photograph_Guidelines.pdf 
Visa application form : 
o 
o 
Completed and signed by the applicant, if applicable, authorization of third person. 
If the applicant is a minor: visa application signed by legal guardian/s and if applicable, authorization of third person. 
(Proving the custody of a minor child, divorce papers, death certificate etc.). 
Introduction letter : 
o 
o 
o 
o 
Letter (original document), needs to be on a business letterhead from the employer/company. 
Letter needs to be signed and stamped by HR or directorate, mentioning position and duration of service. 
‘No objection statement’ regarding the planned trip to Switzerland/Schengen States. 
Mentioning of travel dates and purpose of trip. 
Travel Insurance : 
o Please refer to the list of approved Indian travel insurances: 
https://www.eda.admin.ch/dam/countries/countries-content/india/en/visa/SchengetList-Travel_Insurance_Companies- 
EN.pdf 
o 
o 
Minimum coverage of 30,000 Euro per person. 
Coverage of all expenses which might arise in connection with repatriation for medical reasons, urgent medical attention 
and/or emergency hospital treatment. 
o 
o 
The insurance has to cover the entire stay within the Schengen territory. 
Applicants who wish to apply for a six months or a one year multiple visa are not obliged to provide a 180 (365) day 
coverage. It is sufficient to submit an insurance policy covering the first journey. 
Flight reservation : 
o 
o 
With names of all travelers. 
If applicable proof of intra-Schengen flights, train itinerary or car rental. 
Proof of accommodation in Switzerland / Schengen states : 
o Confirmation of hotel reservations, package tours, advance payments.

Proof of financial status : 
All documents have to be originals, in A4 format, with stamp and signature of Bank. 
o If employed : 
• 
• 
• 
Last 3 months salary slips 
Last 3 months personal bank account statements in which the salary is credited 
Personal ITR (only ITR-V, Indian Income Tax Return Verification Form or Acknowledgement is accepted) for 
the last 2 assessment years. 
o If self-employed and/or company owner : 
• Business registration certificate / GST Registration with annex A & B / Partnership Deed / Proof of proprietorship 
or other proof of ownership 
• 
• 
Last 3 months personal bank statements 
Personal ITR (only ITR-V, Indian Income Tax Return Verification Form or Acknowledgement is 
accepted) for the last 2 assessment years. 
o If retired : 
• 
• 
Personal pension bank statement for the last 3 months. 
Proof of regular income generated by ownership of property or business. 
Families : 
o The visa applications have to be submitted together, only the head of family needs to attach bank statements, personal 
ITR (only ITR-V, Indian Income Tax Return Verification Form or Acknowledgement is accepted), travel insurance 
(mentioning all the names of the insured), flight booking (mentioning all the names of the travelling family members), all 
the hotel confirmations (if all the rooms are under the same booking and under the name of the head of the family). 
o Students above 16 year old : 
Copy of the student college ID card and introduction letter of the School/College/University. 
o 
o 
Children under 18 years old, not travelling with both parents : 
• Consent letter of the non-travelling parent or legal guardian, certified by public notary. 
Children under 18 years old, travelling alone : 
Consent letter of both parents or legal guardians, certified by public notary. • 
o 
o 
In all cases: copy of passport from both parents (legal guardian/s) or PAN card or driving license. 
Visa fees: 
• Adults: EUR 90.00 
• Children between 6 and 12 years old: EUR 45.00 
Children under 6 years old: free of charge
